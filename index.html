About Me

I'm Kirsten (kers-ten) - a software developer with a passion for code and creativity. My experience spans the full stack, from a beautiful & responsive frontend to a secure backend with solutions that are both efficient and elegant. With over three years of self-study, supplemented by 18 weeks of formal cohort training and certification, I've actively honed my skills in software development (including the crucial, undertaught soft skills) Based in Cleveland, Ohio yet happy to connect remotely far and wide. Birding, indulging in the world of design (tech, fashion, interior, etc) make up much of my non-programming free time, which intertwines with my knack for drawing, writing & stories, coming up with quick-witted ideas & letting my imagination do its thing. One of my greatest personal goals is to one day learn some game development skills, and bring to life something that I've been dreaming of for a long time now (stay tuned!)
